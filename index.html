About Me

Welcome! I'm Amy Finch, a seasoned software developer based in Ontario, Canada, with a deep-rooted passion for transforming complex challenges into innovative tech solutions. Holding a Bachelor's degree in Computer Networking and boasting over a decade of expertise in the gaming industry—from engine development to marketing and 3D modeling—I bring a diverse and rich portfolio of skills. My experience stretches beyond gaming; I have honed my abilities in cyber security, full-stack development, and more, working with languages like PHP, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS.


As a creator and maintainer of my own dedicated servers, I manage sites that cater to over 30,000 daily visitors and have contributed to platforms experiencing traffic upwards of 500,000 visitors per day. My professional journey has allowed me to delve into web development, cloud computing, and data analysis, continually pushing the boundaries of technology.


I am actively engaged in the tech community, sharing insights, projects, and updates on LinkedIn and GitHub. On LinkedIn, my profile chronicles my professional adventures and current ventures, while GitHub serves as a repository for my personal projects, demonstrating my prowess across various programming languages and technologies.


Outside the digital realm, I'm an avid scuba diver, traveler, and multimedia enthusiast, with a penchant for volunteering within online communities. As an introvert, I thrive in virtual interactions and have led several online communities, leveraging platforms like Discord to foster engagement and connectivity.


From my beginnings in Ontario to roles at major companies like AWS in Washington, USA, and remote projects for clients in South Africa, my career is a testament to my dedication and relentless pursuit of excellence in the tech industry. Whether I'm developing sophisticated systems or enriching the tech community with my knowledge, I am committed to making a significant impact in the tech world.
